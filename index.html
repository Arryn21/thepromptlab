Four timed activities tonight. Use the timers to keep pace during the workshop.

Activity 01 · ChatGPT

Build Your First Worksheet

Use the RTFC formula to create a practice worksheet for one of your actual units. Ask for an answer key.

  • Pick a topic you're teaching next week
  • Fill in the Worksheet template in the RTFC builder
  • Compare your result with a neighbor
05:00
Activity 02 · Claude

Student-Friendly Explainer

Ask Claude to explain a concept your students always struggle with — at student level, with an analogy.

  • Use the Claude tab in AI Arena
  • Try the Student-Friendly Explainer prompt
  • Then try the "dual-audience version" from the library
08:00
Activity 03 · Gemini

Classroom Visual Generator

Generate a classroom poster or flyer using Gemini's image generation. No design skills needed.

  • Open gemini.google.com in a new tab
